  • PAN of LLP

  • PAN + Aadhaar of all designated partners
  • DPIN certificates

  • Bank statements 12 months
📋Authorization
  • Partners' resolution for the loan
  • MCA annual filings (Form 8 & Form 11)

  • PAN of Firm
  • Partnership Deed (notarized)
  • GST / Shop & Est. certificate

  • PAN + Aadhaar of all partners
  • Personal ITR of major partners

  • Firm ITR 2–3 years

  • Bank statements 12 months
📋Authorization
  • All partners' consent letter for loan
  • Personal guarantee of all partners

  • PAN of proprietor
  • Aadhaar Card
  • GST Registration
  • Udyam / MSME registration

  • ITR 2–3 years (business income)

  • GST returns 6–12 months

  • Declaration & list of coparceners

  • HUF ITR 2–3 years

📋Authorization
  • Consent of all coparceners
  • Karta's authorization letter
⚠ Personal guarantee of Karta typically mandatory.

  • PAN of Trust / Society
